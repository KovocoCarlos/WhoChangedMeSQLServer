Question 1 — Problem Existence
"Do you currently audit your SQL Server environment?"
Prompts if they hesitate:

"I mean things like tracking who accessed data, who changed a table, login activity, permission changes — anything like that."
"This could be manual, automated, using native SQL Server tools, or a third-party product."

Why it matters: This is your qualifier. If they say no, you're uncovering a latent market. If yes, you move deeper.
